Privacy Policy for BudPlan

Effective date: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the BudPlan mobile application ("BudPlan," "the app," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use it. We have designed BudPlan to keep your data on your own device and to collect as little information as possible. By using BudPlan, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Summary

  • BudPlan is a free personal organizer app for tracking receivables and debts, subscriptions, payments, checklists, notes, and reminders.
  • Your content (payments, receivables, subscriptions, checklists, notes, and calendar entries) is stored locally on your device using a local database (SQLite). We do not host it on our servers.
  • There is no account, no login, and no cloud sync. We do not request your name, email address, or any similar personal information.
  • BudPlan is free and contains no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
  • BudPlan shows ads through Google AdMob, which may process certain device data as described below.
  • We do not use Firebase, analytics SDKs, or logging, and we do not maintain our own user profiles.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you create in the app

When you use BudPlan, you may enter information such as receivable and debt records, subscription details, payment entries, checklist items, notes, and calendar reminders. This content is stored only in a local database (SQLite) on your device. It is not transmitted to us, and we have no access to it. If you delete the app, all of this locally stored data is removed from your device.

2.2 Information collected automatically by third-party services

BudPlan itself does not collect personal identifiers, but the third-party services we integrate may automatically collect certain technical information when you use the app. This is described in Section 3.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob (advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Ads are placed so as not to disrupt your experience. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, IP address, ad interaction data, and other device or usage information. This processing is governed by Google's policies. You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

On supported platforms we request your consent or tracking preference before personalized advertising is used:

  • iOS: Through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, we ask for your permission before any tracking across apps and websites takes place. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant.
  • Android / EEA & UK: Through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP), we present a consent form where required so you can choose how your data is used for advertising.

3.2 Notifications

BudPlan can send you local, in-app notifications to remind you about the activities scheduled on your calendar for the relevant day. These notifications are generated on your device from the content you have entered and are not sent through, or shared with, any external server.

3.3 Update and configuration checks

BudPlan may contact a remote configuration source to check whether a newer version of the app is available. This request transmits only standard technical information needed to complete the request and does not include your personal content.

3.4 App rating

BudPlan uses the official in-app review feature provided by the App Store (Apple) and Google Play (Google) to let you optionally rate the app. This feature is handled entirely by the respective platform; we do not receive or store any personal information through it.

4. How Information Is Used

Information is used only for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core features of the app (receivable and debt tracking, subscription tracking, payments, checklists, notes, calendar, and reminders), processed locally on your device.
  • To display advertising and, where applicable, to respect your advertising consent or tracking choices.
  • To check for app updates.

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Your in-app content is stored locally on your device in a local database (SQLite) and remains there until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app. Because we do not operate servers that store your content, we cannot retrieve or restore your data if it is lost or deleted. Data processed by third-party services (such as AdMob) is retained according to those providers' own policies.

6. Children's Privacy

BudPlan is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. While the app contains no objectionable content and may carry a low age rating in app stores, it is not specifically designed for or targeted to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertising service described above is provided by a third party subject to its own terms. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used by the app, please contact us so we can assist where possible.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • Advertising preferences: You can adjust your tracking preference (iOS) in your device settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking, and you can reset your advertising identifier on both iOS and Android in your device settings.
  • Your content: You can view, edit, or delete the records, subscriptions, checklists, notes, and calendar entries you create directly within the app, or remove all of it by uninstalling the app.
  • Regional rights: Depending on where you live (for example under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have additional rights regarding personal data processed by the third-party services described above. Those rights can be exercised through the respective providers' privacy tools.

8. International Data Transfers

The third-party services used by BudPlan may process data on servers located in countries other than your own. Where this occurs, it is handled by those providers under their respective privacy frameworks and safeguards.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
